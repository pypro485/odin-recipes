lasagna

lasagna is amazing

lasagna, an amazing dish made with the finest beef and cheeses. After 3 hours and 15 minutes of work you can get the most delicous food.

If you want to make this you just need some ingredients. Meat, Onions and garlic, Tomato, sugar, spices, noodles, cheeses, and one fine egg

ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. let the lasagna rest before serving
